Dine 'N Charge

Dine while your vehicle charges. We will help you find a charging station and show you restaurants in the area, just follow the instructions below.

Instructions:
1. Please select the area on the map where you would like a charging station. Highly recommended stations are in green.
2. Select a location pin for your charging station.
3. Press submit. This will bring up restaurant choices in the area.
4. Select a restaurant or press refresh to start over.
